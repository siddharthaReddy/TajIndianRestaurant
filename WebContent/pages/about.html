About Us

India, the land of the enchanted, with its varied cultural practices, is tied together by the invisible commonality of its exotic cuisine. It is celebrated for the variety of its dishes based on the artful and intelligent combination of spices developed over many centuries. Every dish is created from a unique blend of flavorings, spices with the purity of Mother Nature.

Taj Indian restaurant is a remarkable blend of many of these culinary techniques, ingredients and traditions. The Taj restaurant is known for its flavor, savory sauces enriched by nuts, cream and fruits.Taj Indian Restaurant has over 25 years in business serving fine Indian cuisine in a warm, elegant atmosphere. We provide the best lunch buffet in town. Please do come and enjoy your favorite Indian cuisine in the warm and welcoming ambience of our dining room.
